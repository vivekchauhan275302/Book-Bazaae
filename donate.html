Your Impact

Every book donation creates ripples of knowledge and inspiration, touching lives across communities.

Education

Your donated books help students access educational materials they might not otherwise afford.

Environment

Reusing books reduces waste and saves trees, contributing to environmental conservation.

Community

Shared books build community connections and promote literacy across all age groups.

How to Donate Books

1

Prepare Your Books

Clean your books and make sure they're in good condition. We accept most genres and reading levels.

2

Drop Off at a Collection Point

Bring your books to any Book Bazaar store or one of our partner libraries listed below.

3

Schedule a Pickup

For bulk donations (more than 20 books), we can arrange a free pickup from your location.
